Question 3medium

Which word correctly fills the blank? 'She was completely ___ by the complexity of the task.'

  1. Overwhelmed
  2. Overwhelming
  3. Overwhelm
  4. Overwheming
Show answer

Answer: Overwhelmed

'Overwhelmed' (past participle used as adjective) describes how a person feels: 'She was overwhelmed.' Compare: 'The task was overwhelming' (present participle as adjective = describes the thing causing the feeling). Person = -ed adjective; thing causing feeling = -ing adjective. 'She felt overwhelmed (not overwhelming).'

Question 4hard

The literary technique 'in medias res' refers to:

  1. Starting a story from the very beginning
  2. Beginning a story in the middle of the action, without prior explanation
  3. Ending a story abruptly
  4. A flashback technique
Show answer

Answer: Beginning a story in the middle of the action, without prior explanation

'In medias res' (Latin: 'into the middle of things'): a narrative technique where the story begins in the middle of the action, with background filled in later through flashbacks or exposition. Used in Homer's Iliad and Odyssey, many modern films and novels.
