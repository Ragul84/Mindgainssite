Question 1easy

In a pie chart, a sector represents 25% of the data. Its central angle is:

  1. 90°
  2. 45°
  3. 180°
  4. 120°
Show answer

Answer: 90°

25% of 360° = 0.25×360° = 90°.

Question 2easy

A bar chart shows production: 2019=500, 2020=600, 2021=750. % increase 2019 to 2021:

  1. 50%
  2. 25%
  3. 30%
  4. 40%
Show answer

Answer: 50%

% increase = (750−500)/500×100 = 250/500×100 = 50%.

  1. ₹1,50,000
  2. ₹1,00,000
  3. ₹2,00,000
  4. ₹1,25,000

Answer: ₹1,50,000
