  1. 30th
  2. 31st
  3. 32nd
  4. 33rd

Answer: 33rd

The 2024 Paris Olympics (26 July – 11 August 2024) is the 33rd Summer Olympic Games. Paris has hosted the Olympics three times: 1900, 1924, and 2024.
