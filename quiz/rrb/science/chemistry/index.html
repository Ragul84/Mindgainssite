Question 3medium

The chemical name of 'washing soda' is:

  1. Sodium bicarbonate
  2. Sodium carbonate (Na2CO3·10H2O)
  3. Calcium carbonate
  4. Potassium carbonate
Show answer

Answer: Sodium carbonate (Na2CO3·10H2O)

Washing soda is hydrated sodium carbonate (Na2CO3·10H2O). Baking soda is sodium bicarbonate (NaHCO3).

Question 4medium

Rusting of iron is a chemical process called:

  1. Sublimation
  2. Corrosion (oxidation)
  3. Reduction
  4. Decomposition
Show answer

Answer: Corrosion (oxidation)

Rusting is the corrosion of iron. It is an oxidation reaction: 4Fe + 3O2 + 6H2O → 4Fe(OH)3 (hydrated iron oxide). Moisture and oxygen are needed.

Question 5medium

Which enzyme in the saliva begins the digestion of starch?

  1. Pepsin
  2. Trypsin
  3. Amylase (Ptyalin)
  4. Lipase
Show answer

Answer: Amylase (Ptyalin)

Salivary amylase (ptyalin) begins the chemical digestion of starch in the mouth, breaking it into maltose.
