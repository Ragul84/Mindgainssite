Question 15medium

The 'Law of Gravitation' was proposed by:

  1. Albert Einstein
  2. Galileo Galilei
  3. Isaac Newton
  4. Nicolaus Copernicus
Show answer

Answer: Isaac Newton

Sir Isaac Newton formulated the Universal Law of Gravitation (1687): F = Gm1m2/r², stating that every object attracts every other object with a force proportional to their masses and inversely proportional to the square of the distance between them.

Question 19hard
