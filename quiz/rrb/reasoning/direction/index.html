Question 1easy

If North is 0°, what direction is 180°?

  1. East
  2. West
  3. South
  4. Northeast
Show answer

Answer: South

Starting from North (0°), going clockwise: East=90°, South=180°, West=270°.

Question 2easy

A man walks 3 km East, then 4 km North. How far is he from the starting point?

  1. 5 km
  2. 7 km
  3. 6 km
  4. 4 km
Show answer

Answer: 5 km

Using Pythagoras: √(3² + 4²) = √(9+16) = √25 = 5 km.
