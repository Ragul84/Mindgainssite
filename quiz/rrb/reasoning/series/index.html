Question 1easy

Find the next number in the series: 2, 6, 12, 20, 30, ?

  1. 40
  2. 42
  3. 44
  4. 46
Show answer

Answer: 42

Pattern: differences are 4, 6, 8, 10, 12. Next = 30+12 = 42. Or n(n+1): 1×2=2, 2×3=6, 3×4=12, 4×5=20, 5×6=30, 6×7=42.

Question 2easy

Find the missing number in the series: 1, 3, 5, 7, 9, ?

  1. 9
  2. 11
  3. 13
  4. 15
Show answer

Answer: 11

The series is an Arithmetic Progression (AP) with a common difference of +2: 1 + 2 = 3 3 + 2 = 5 5 + 2 = 7 7 + 2 = 9 Therefore, the missing number is 9 + 2 = 11.
