Question 11medium

Show answer

Answer: Primitive subsistence farming

Primitive subsistence farming, also known as 'slash and burn' or 'jhum' cultivation, is a traditional method where farmers clear a patch of land by felling trees and burning the vegetation. Question 34medium

  2. Over-exploitation and mismanagement of water resources
  3. Absence of large rivers
  4. High evaporation rates due to desert climate
Show answer

Answer: Over-exploitation and mismanagement of water resources

India has many large rivers, and high evaporation rates are specific to certain regions, not a nationwide primary cause of scarcity despite ample rainfall.
