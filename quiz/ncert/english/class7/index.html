Choose the correct passive voice for the sentence: 'The gardener waters the plants daily.'

  1. The plants are watered daily by the gardener.
  2. The plants were watered daily by the gardener.
  3. The plants is watered daily by the gardener.
  4. The gardener is watering the plants daily.
Show answer

Answer: The plants are watered daily by the gardener.

In active voice, the structure is Subject + Verb + Object. In passive voice, it becomes Object + 'be' verb (according to tense) + Past Participle of Verb + by + Subject. Here, 'waters' is in simple present tense, so 'are watered' is used. 'The plants' is the object, and 'the gardener' is the subject.
