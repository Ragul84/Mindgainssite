Question 3medium

Convert the following sentence into passive voice: 'The children are playing cricket.'

  2. Cricket was played by the children.
  3. Cricket is being played by the children.
  4. Cricket has been played by the children.
Show answer

Answer: Cricket is being played by the children.

To convert an active voice sentence in the present continuous tense (subject + is/am/are + verb-ing + object) to passive voice, the structure is: object + is/am/are + being + past participle of the verb + by + subject. Here, 'cricket' is the object, 'is' is the auxiliary verb, 'being' is added, and 'played' is the past participle of 'playing'.
