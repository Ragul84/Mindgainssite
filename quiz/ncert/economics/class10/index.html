Question 3medium

Which of the following is a major disadvantage of the barter system?

  1. Requires a double coincidence of wants
  2. Facilitates easy exchange of goods
  3. Promotes specialization
  4. Allows for deferred payments
Show answer

Answer: Requires a double coincidence of wants

The barter system involves direct exchange of goods without the use of money. Its major disadvantage is the 'double coincidence of wants,' meaning both parties must simultaneously desire what the other has to offer. This makes transactions difficult and inefficient, unlike money which acts as a medium of exchange.

Answer: Source of employment
