The Tamil Nadu Industrial Development Corporation (TIDCO) was established to:

  1. Promote and facilitate industrial development in Tamil Nadu — set up joint ventures, provide equity participation, and develop industrial estates
  2. Provide agricultural loans
  3. Manage ports
  4. Build roads
Show answer

Answer: Promote and facilitate industrial development in Tamil Nadu — set up joint ventures, provide equity participation, and develop industrial estates

TIDCO (1965): TN's state government industrial promotion company. Functions: promotes investment, sets up joint ventures with private companies, participates in equity of industrial projects, and develops sector-specific parks. Examples of TIDCO JVs: TANCEM (cement), TANTRANSCO (power), TIDEL Park (IT park in Chennai).
