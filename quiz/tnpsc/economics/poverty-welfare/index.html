The 'poverty trap' is a situation where:

  1. Poor people cannot escape poverty because they lack resources to invest in health, education, or productive assets — low income → low savings → low investment → low income (vicious cycle)

  3. Poverty is self-fulfilling prophecy due to geography
  4. Lack of democracy causes poverty
Show answer

Answer: Poor people cannot escape poverty because they lack resources to invest in health, education, or productive assets — low income → low savings → low investment → low income (vicious cycle)

Question 22hard

The term 'terms of trade' in international economics refers to:

  1. Ratio of export price index to import price index — if terms of trade improve (exports become relatively more expensive), country can import more with same exports; important for commodity exporters
  2. Trade agreements
  3. Trade volumes
  4. Trade deficits
Show answer

Answer: Ratio of export price index to import price index — if terms of trade improve (exports become relatively more expensive), country can import more with same exports; important for commodity exporters

Terms of Trade (ToT) = (Export Price Index / Import Price Index) × 100. If ToT rises: country's exports buy more imports → welfare improves. If ToT falls: exports buy fewer imports → welfare falls. Important for developing countries exporting primary commodities (prices volatile, tend to fall relative to manufactured goods — Prebisch-Singer hypothesis). India: ToT typically negative due to oil imports.
