Question 10medium

Trade winds blow from:

  1. Sub-tropical high pressure belts towards the equator (from NE in N hemisphere, SE in S hemisphere)
  2. Equator towards poles
  3. Polar regions towards equator
  4. West to east everywhere
Show answer

Answer: Sub-tropical high pressure belts towards the equator (from NE in N hemisphere, SE in S hemisphere)

Trade winds blow from the sub-tropical high pressure belts (around 30°N and 30°S) towards the equatorial low pressure belt. In the N hemisphere, they blow from NE; in the S hemisphere, from SE.
