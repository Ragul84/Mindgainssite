Question 16hard

  1. Cyclonic rainfall
  2. Convectional rainfall
  3. Orographic (relief) rainfall
  4. Frontal rainfall
Show answer

Answer: Orographic (relief) rainfall

In orographic rainfall, moisture-laden winds rise over a mountain range, cool, and precipitate on the windward side. The leeward side remains dry — this is the 'rain shadow' region. E.g., Shillong is on the windward side; the Deccan Plateau is in the rain shadow of the Western Ghats.
