Question 24medium

The Mughal land revenue system during Akbar's reign was called:

  1. Iqta
  2. Zamindari
  3. Dahsala or Zabti system (introduced by Todar Mal)
  4. Mahalwari
Show answer

Answer: Dahsala or Zabti system (introduced by Todar Mal)
