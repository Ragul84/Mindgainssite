Question 3easy

The greatest ruler of the Vijayanagara Empire was:

  1. Harihara I
  2. Deva Raya II
  3. Krishna Deva Raya
  4. Rama Raya
Show answer

Answer: Krishna Deva Raya

Krishna Deva Raya (1509–1529 CE) was the greatest Vijayanagara ruler. He defeated Bijapur, Bidar, Golconda, and the Zamorin of Calicut. He was a poet (wrote Amuktamalyada in Telugu) and patron of Ashtadiggajas (eight court poets).
