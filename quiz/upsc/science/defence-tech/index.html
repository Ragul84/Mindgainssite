  3. Cruise missiles
  4. Naval torpedoes
Show answer

Answer: Surface-to-surface ballistic missiles with nuclear capability — Agni I (700 km), Agni V (5,000+ km, ICBM class)

Agni missiles (DRDO): Agni-I (700 km range), Agni-II (2,000 km), Agni-III (3,500 km), Agni-IV (4,000 km), Agni-V (5,000+ km, MIRVed).
