Question 6medium

'Subduction zone' is where:

  1. A denser tectonic plate slides under a lighter plate (causes earthquakes and volcanoes)
  2. Two plates move apart
  3. Two continental plates collide
  4. A plate cracks open
Show answer

Answer: A denser tectonic plate slides under a lighter plate (causes earthquakes and volcanoes)

A subduction zone is where an oceanic plate (denser) slides under a continental or another oceanic plate. This causes deep-focus earthquakes, trenches (like Mariana Trench), and volcanic arcs (Ring of Fire).
