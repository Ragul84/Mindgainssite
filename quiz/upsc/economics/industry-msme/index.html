The concept of 'Comparative Advantage' in international trade was developed by:

  3. John Keynes
  4. Paul Krugman (New Trade Theory)

Answer: David Ricardo (1817, 'On the Principles of Political Economy and Taxation') — countries should specialize in producing goods they produce relatively more efficiently, even if less efficient absolutely
