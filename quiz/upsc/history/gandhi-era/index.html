Question 4hard

The 'Poona Pact' (1932) was signed between Ambedkar and Gandhi on the issue of:

  1. Partition of India
  2. Separate electorates for Dalits (Depressed Classes)
  3. Provincial autonomy
  4. Temple entry for untouchables

Answer: Separate electorates for Dalits (Depressed Classes)
