# TNPSC 100-Day Study Plan

## BLOCK 1: GENERAL TAMIL (Days 1–14)
D1: எழுத்திலக்கணம் — 247 letters(uyir 12+mey 18+uyirmey 216+aytham 1), vowel types, consonant types, pronunciation rules
D2: சொல் இலக்கணம் — 4 word types(peyar/vinai/idai/uri), morphology, word formation, prefixes & suffixes
D3: வேற்றுமை — 8 case markers with uruppugal & meanings(எழுவாய்/செயப்படுபொருள்/கருவி/நோக்கம்/இடம்/தனிமை/உடனிகழ்வு/விளி)
D4: தொடர் இலக்கணம் — Sentence types(simple/compound/complex), clause structure, subject-predicate, verbal noun
D5: அணி இலக்கணம் — Figures of speech(உவமை/உருவகம்/பேரணி/சொல்லணி) + Grammar traps in TNPSC
D6: Sangam Age — 3 Sangams(Madurai/Kapatapuram/Madurai), Thinai system(5 landscapes+akam/puram), Ettuthokai, Pattuppattu
D7: REVISION — Days 1–6 + Grammar MCQ drill(30 questions)
D8: திருக்குறள் — 1330 kurals/133 adhikarams/3 sections(Aram-Porul-Inbam), Key adhikarams(TNPSC prescribed 25), Valluvar biography
D9: Sangam Literature Deep — 18 Major texts(Purananuru,Akananuru,Kalittokai...), 18 Minor texts(பதினெண்கீழ்க்கணக்கு: Naaladiyar,Naanmanikadikai...)
D10: Post-Sangam & Epic Literature — Silappatikaram(Ilango Adigal,Kannagi), Manimekalai(Seethalai Saathanar), Cilappatikaram geography
D11: Medieval Tamil Literature — Kamba Ramayanam(Kambar,12th century), Periya Puranam(Sekkizhar), Tevaram(Nayanmars), Divya Prabandham(Alvars)
D12: Modern Tamil Literature — Bharathiyar(nationalist poetry), Bharathidasan(Dravidian poetry), Pudhukkavithai movement, Contemporary writers
D13: Tamil Classical Status & Language Policy — Classical language(2004), Anti-Hindi agitation(1937+1965), Tamil Nadu's language position
D14: REVISION — Days 8–13 + Literature identification MCQ drill(30 questions)

## BLOCK 2: TN HISTORY — ANCIENT (Days 15–21)
D15: Archaeological Sites — Keezhadi(Sivaganga,Vaigai,6th BCE,Tamil-Brahmi,urban civilization), Adichanallur(Thoothukudi,Tamirabarani,urn burials,Iron Age,Alexander Rea), Arikamedu(Puducherry,Roman trade,amphorae+coins,Mortimer Wheeler 1945), Kodumanal(Erode,Noyyal,gem+bead industry)
D16: Sangam-era Tamil Kingdoms — Chera(Vanji capital,spice trade,Senguttuvan), Chola(Uraiyur→Kaveripattinam,Karikala Chola,Kallanai), Pandya(Madurai,pearl fishery,Korkai)
D17: Pallava Dynasty — Kanchipuram capital, Mahabalipuram(Pancha Rathas,Shore Temple), Mahendravarman I(cave temples,Mattavilasa Prahasana), Narasimhavarman I(Mamalla)
D18: Medieval Chola Empire — Vijayalaya(848CE,founder), Raja Raja I(Brihadeeswara Thanjavur,Navy,Sri Lanka+Maldives), Rajendra I(Gangaikondacholapuram,Bay of Bengal campaign), Kudavolai system(Uttaramerur inscription)
D19: Pallava & Pandya Art — Dravidian temple style(gopuram,vimana,mandapam), Rock-cut temples(Mahabalipuram), Bronze casting(Nataraja=Chola), Monolithic Rathas
D20: Post-Chola Period — Pandya revival(Marco Polo visited), Malik Kafur invasion(1311), Later Pandyas, Transition to Vijayanagara period
D21: REVISION — Days 15–20 + TN Ancient History MCQ drill

## BLOCK 3: TN HISTORY — MEDIEVAL (Days 22–28)
D22: Vijayanagara Empire — Founded 1336(Harihara+Bukka,Sangama dynasty), Hampi capital, Krishnadevaraya(greatest ruler), Protected TN from Delhi Sultanate, Fall=Battle of Talikota(1565)
D23: Nayak Period — Governors under Vijayanagara→Independent after 1565. Madurai Nayaks(Thirumalai Nayak=Mahal+Meenakshi Temple expansion). Thanjavur Nayaks. Gingee Nayaks
D24: Thanjavur Marathas — Ekoji/Venkoji(Shivaji's half-brother,captured Thanjavur 1674), Serfoji II(1798-1832,Saraswati Mahal Library,arts promotion)
D25: TN Temple Architecture — Dravidian style(gopuram+vimana+mandapam+tank+enclosure walls), Chola(Brihadeeswara,Gangaikondacholapuram), Nayak(Meenakshi,Ramanaathaswamy), Rajagopuram heights
D26: Advent of Europeans in TN — Portuguese(1498,Vasco da Gama,Kozhikode), Dutch(Pulicat colony), French(Pondicherry/Puducherry,Dupleix), British(1639 Madraspatnam/Fort St.George)
D27: Anglo-French Rivalry to British Supremacy — Carnatic Wars(1st,2nd,3rd), Battle of Wandiwash(1760,British defeated French), Clive vs Dupleix strategy
D28: REVISION — Days 22–27 + TN Medieval History MCQ drill

## BLOCK 4: TN HISTORY — FREEDOM MOVEMENT (Days 29–35)
D29: Early Uprisings Timeline Grid — Puli Thevar(1757,Tirunelveli,first defier)+Velu Nachiyar(1780,Veeramangai,Udaiyaal Padai,Kuyili=first suicide bombing)+Kattabomman(1799,Panchalankurichi,hanged)+Marudhu Brothers(1801,first Proclamation of Independence,executed)
D30: Periyar E.V. Ramasamy — Born 1879 Coimbatore,died 1973. Left Congress 1925. Self-Respect Movement(1925). Justice Party→DK(Dravidar Kazhagam 1944). Anti-caste,anti-religion,women's rights,widow remarriage. UNESCO="Prophet of New Age"
D31: Justice Party to DMK — Justice Party(1916,T.M.Nair,non-Brahmin), Annadurai(DMK 1949,broke from DK), DMK wins 1967(first non-Congress CM of TN,first non-Congress govt in any major state)
D32: Post-Independence TN Political History — Annadurai(1967-1969,died in office), Karunanidhi(DMK), MGR(broke from DMK,AIADMK 1972,CM 1977-87), Jayalalithaa(CM multiple terms,died 2016)
D33: Social Reforms in TN — Anti-Hindi agitation(1937 under British,1965 under Congress), 69% reservation(BC27%+MBC20%+SC18%+ST1%+others,9th Schedule protection), Temple entry(1939,Periyar)
D34: TN's National Leaders — V.O.Chidambaram Pillai(Swadeshi Steam Navigation Co), Subramania Bharati(poet+nationalist), Rajaji(C.Rajagopalachari,last Governor-General,Chakravarti Rajagopalachari)
D35: REVISION — Days 29–34 + Freedom Movement TN PYQ drill

## BLOCK 5: TN GEOGRAPHY (Days 36–42)
D36: TN Physical Geography — 38 districts(after 2022), 4 natural regions(Eastern Ghats+Deccan plateau+Plains+Coastal), Borders(AP+Karnataka+Kerala+sea), Highest peak=Doddabetta(2637m,Nilgiris), South India highest=Anamudi(2695m,Kerala)
D37: TN Rivers & Dams — Cauvery(Karnataka origin,dispute)+Vaigai(Dindigul,Vaigai Dam)+Tamirabarani(Tirunelveli)+Palar+Pennaiyar. Dams: Kallanai/Grand Anicut(world's oldest,Karikala Chola,Thanjavur)+Mettur/Stanley Reservoir(1934,Salem,largest in TN)
D38: TN Climate & Agriculture Zones — SW Monsoon(June-Sept,W.Ghats+N.TN), NE Monsoon(Oct-Dec,coastal TN 60% rain). Zones: Cauvery delta(rice)+Coimbatore(cotton+coconut)+Tirunelveli(banana+pearl millet)+Nilgiris(tea+coffee)
D39: TN Agriculture & GI Products — Rice Bowl=Cauvery delta(Thanjavur+Tiruvarur+Nagapattinam). Erode=Turmeric City. GI tags: Kanchipuram Silk,Coimbatore Wet Grinder,Erode Turmeric,Nilgiris Tea,Thanjavur Painting,Madurai Malli. ~39,000 irrigation tanks(highest in India)
D40: TN Natural Resources & Forests — Minerals: Lignite(Neyveli,Cuddalore)+Magnesite(Salem)+Limestone+Granite. Forest types: Tropical dry evergreen(E.Ghats)+Shola forests(Nilgiris). NLC(Neyveli Lignite Corporation)
D41: TN Environment & Protected Areas — State animal=Nilgiri Tahr,State bird=Emerald Dove,State tree=Palmyra,State flower=Gloriosa superba. 5 Tiger Reserves+5 National Parks+Gulf of Mannar(first marine BR,S/SE Asia,Dugong). Nilgiri BR=India's first(1986)
D42: REVISION — Days 36–41 + TN Geography MCQ drill

## BLOCK 6: INDIAN POLITY FOR TNPSC (Days 43–56)
D43: Constitution Overview — Parts(25),Articles(448+),Schedules(12),Constituent Assembly(1946-1949),Key Committees(Drafting=Ambedkar,Union Powers=Nehru)
D44: 12 Schedules Drill — 1st(States+UTs) 4th(RS seats) 5th(Scheduled Areas) 6th(Tribal Areas NE) 7th(Union+State+Concurrent Lists) 8th(22 languages) 9th(Judicial review exempt,TN 69% reservation here) 10th(Anti-defection) 11th(PR 29 subjects) 12th(ULB 18 subjects)
D45: Fundamental Rights — Art 12-18(Equality),19-22(Freedom,Art 19 can be suspended=external emergency only,Art 20+21 can NEVER be suspended),23-35(Exploitation,Religion,Minorities,Remedies). Art 32=heart+soul(Ambedkar)
D46: DPSP & Fundamental Duties — Art 36-51(not justiciable): Socialist(39,42),Gandhian(40,46,47,48),Liberal(44=UCC,50=judiciary separation). Art 51A=11 FDs(42nd Amdt added 10,86th added 11th)
D47: Parliament — LS(543 elected)+RS(238+12 nominated by President). Quorum=1/10th. Money Bill(Art 110,only LS,RS delays 14 days max). Joint Sitting(Art 108,Speaker of LS presides). Ordinance(Art 123=President,Art 213=Governor)
D48: President & PM — Art 52-72: Electoral College(elected MPs+MLAs ONLY,no nominated,no governors). Art 72 pardoning(all offences+court martial+death penalty). PM Art 75(collective responsibility to LS). CoM=Art 74-75
D49: REVISION — Days 43–48 + Indian Polity for TNPSC PYQ drill
D50: TN State Government — Governor(Art 153,appointed by President,at pleasure of President,Art 161=state pardons). CM=leader of majority. TN Vidhan Sabha=234 seats(Anglo-Indian abolished 104th Amdt 2019). No Vidhan Parishad in TN
D51: TN District Administration — District Collector(IAS,head of district,revenue+law+order+elections+disaster). Revenue structure: District→Revenue Division→Taluk(Tahsildar)→Revenue Village(VAO=Village Administrative Officer)
D52: TN Panchayati Raj — 73rd Amdt(1992)→TN Panchayats Act 1994. 3 tiers: District Panchayat+Panchayat Union(Block)+Gram Panchayat. Women reservation=50%(TN exceeds constitutional minimum of 33%). TN State Election Commission conducts elections
D53: Urban Local Bodies — 12 Municipal Corporations(Chennai/GCC=established 1688 British,one of India's oldest+Coimbatore+Madurai+Trichy+Salem+Tirunelveli+Tiruppur+Vellore+Erode+Thoothukudi+Thanjavur+Dindigul). 74th Amdt(1992,18 functions to ULBs,Ward Committees)
D54: Elections & Constitutional Bodies — ECI(Art 324,CEC=impeachment only,NOTA introduced 2013,EVM first use=1982 Kerala). Voting=statutory right(not FR). Finance Commission(Art 280,every 5 yrs). CAG(Art 148)
D55: Key Constitutional Amendments — 42nd(1976,Socialist+Secular+Integrity+DPSP over FR)+52nd(1985,anti-defection 10th Schedule)+61st(1989,voting age 18)+73rd/74th(1992,local bodies)+86th(2002,RTE Art 21A)+101st(GST)+103rd(EWS 10%)+104th(Anglo-Indians abolished)
D56: REVISION — Days 50–55 + Constitutional Articles rapid-fire test

## BLOCK 7: SCIENCE — SAMACHEER (Days 57–70)
D57: Physics — Force & Motion: Newton's 3 Laws,F=ma,Momentum=mv,Impulse=F×t,KE=½mv²,PE=mgh,Law of Conservation of Energy
D58: Physics — Light & Sound: Speed of light=3×10⁸m/s,Speed of sound=343m/s,Reflection+Refraction+Total Internal Reflection(optical fibre),Convex lens=hypermetropia,Concave=myopia,Convex mirror=rear-view,Echo(17m min distance)
D59: Physics — Electricity: V=IR(Ohm's Law),P=VI=I²R,Series(R adds,current same),Parallel(1/R sums,voltage same=household wiring),Transformer(AC only,V₁/V₂=N₁/N₂),Oersted(current→magnet),Faraday(changing B→EMF)
D60: Physics — Heat & Modern: 0°C=273K,Specific heat water=4200J/kg°C(highest),Latent heat(phase change,no temp change),Conduction(solids)+Convection(liquids+gases)+Radiation(no medium needed)
D61: Chemistry — Atomic structure: Atomic number=protons,Mass number=protons+neutrons,Isotopes(same element,diff neutrons),Periodic table(Mendeleev+Moseley correction=atomic number basis)
D62: Chemistry — Reactions: Acids(pH<7,turn litmus red,HCl+H₂SO₄)+Bases(pH>7,turn litmus blue,NaOH)+Salts(neutralization:HCl+NaOH→NaCl+H₂O). Common chemicals: NaHCO₃=baking soda,Na₂CO₃=washing soda,CaOCl₂=bleaching powder
D63: REVISION — Days 57–62 + Physics+Chemistry PYQ drill
D64: Biology — Cell: Cell theory,Prokaryote vs Eukaryote,Cell organelles(mitochondria=powerhouse,chloroplast=photosynthesis,ribosome=protein synthesis,nucleus=control center), Cell division(Mitosis=growth,Meiosis=sexual reproduction)
D65: Biology — Plant Kingdom: Photosynthesis(6CO₂+6H₂O+light→C₆H₁₂O₆+6O₂),Chlorophyll,Stomata,Transpiration,Plant nutrition types(autotroph/heterotroph/parasite/saprophyte)
D66: Biology — Human Digestive & Circulatory: Digestion(mouth→esophagus→stomach→small intestine=main absorption→large intestine=water). Blood types(A,B,AB,O),Universal donor=O-,Universal recipient=AB+,RBC=120 days,WBC=immunity,Platelets=clotting
D67: Biology — Nervous System: Cerebrum(voluntary+thinking),Cerebellum(balance+coordination),Medulla oblongata(involuntary:breathing+heartbeat). Reflex arc(spinal cord,bypasses brain). Neuron:Dendrites(receive)→Cell body→Axon(transmit). Eye:Rods(dim/night)+Cones(colour,fovea)
D68: Biology — Endocrine System: Pituitary=Master gland(GH+TSH+ACTH). Thyroid(T3,T4,needs iodine,deficiency=Goitre). Adrenal(Adrenaline=fight/flight). Pancreas(Beta cells=Insulin lowers glucose,Alpha cells=Glucagon raises glucose). Diabetes=Type1(no insulin)+Type2(resistance)
D69: Biology — Reproductive System & Vitamins: Fertilization=Fallopian tube(NOT uterus). Implantation=Uterus. Ovulation=Day 14. Vitamins:A(night blindness),B1(beriberi),B3(pellagra),B12(anaemia),C(scurvy),D(rickets/osteomalacia),K(blood clotting). Iron=anaemia. Iodine=Goitre
D70: REVISION — Days 64–69 + Samacheer Science PYQ drill
