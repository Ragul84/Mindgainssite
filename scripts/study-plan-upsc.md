# UPSC 100-Day Study Plan (V12.0 - Optimized)

## BLOCK 1: POLITY (Days 1–21)
*Focus: Progressive Stamina Loading (30-min PYQ Sprints starting D7)*
D1: Constitution — Parts, Articles, Schedules, Constituent Assembly
D2: 12 Schedules (all memorized)
D3: Preamble + Citizenship (Art 5–11, Act 1955, CAA 2019)
D4: FR Block 1: Art 12–18 — Equality Rights **[LONG DAY - 25m]**
D5: FR Block 2: Art 19–22 — Freedoms, Life & Arrest
D6: FR Block 3: Art 23–35 — Exploitation, Religion, Minorities, Art 32
D7: **REVISION 1** — Recite Schedules + FR test + **Time-boxed PYQ (30 min)**
D8: DPSP Art 36–51 — Socialistic, Gandhian, Liberal + Minerva Mills
D9: Fundamental Duties Art 51A + Key Amendments Map (42, 44, 52, 61, 73, 74, 86)
D10: Parliament Art 79–122 — Houses, Quorum, Sessions, Joint Sitting
D11: Parliament — Bills (Money/Finance), Majority types, Ordinance
D12: President Art 52–72 — Electoral College, Veto, Pardoning
D13: PM & CoM (Art 74-75) + Governor & State Legislature
D14: **REVISION 2** — 7th Schedule drill + **Time-boxed PYQ (45 min)**
D15: Centre–State Relations — Legis/Admin/Fin + Sarkaria Commission
D16: Emergency Provisions — Art 352, 356, 360
D17: Constitutional Bodies — ECI, UPSC, Fin Comm, CAG, AG
D18: Local Government — 73rd & 74th Amdt (Panchayats & Municipalities)
D19: Constitutional Amendments Art 368 + Basic Structure Doctrine
D20: Governance Bodies — RTI 2005, Lokpal, CVC, NHRC
D21: **REVISION 3** — Full Polity Sprint + **Time-boxed PYQ (60 min)**
