More than a feature

MISA is not just an app tab. It is the public mission: help students, aspirants and lifelong learners across India build the habit of learning every day.

  • Daily learning becomes visible through XP and streaks.
  • State leaderboards turn progress into friendly competition.
  • Public quizzes help learners practice before they enter the app.
  • The long-term goal is a national learning habit, not a one-time download.
