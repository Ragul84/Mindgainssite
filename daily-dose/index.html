Why it exists

Indian learners already have plenty of content. The harder problem is consistency. Daily Dose is designed for the student who wants to learn a little better every day without rebuilding a study plan from scratch.

  • One focused lesson instead of an overwhelming content feed.
  • Quick practice questions to turn reading into recall.
  • Explanations and mistake review so wrong answers become revision.
  • XP and streaks that make daily learning visible.
