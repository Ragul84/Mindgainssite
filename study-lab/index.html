How it works

Students do not always start from a perfect textbook chapter. Sometimes they have a PDF, a video, a rough note or just the topic name. Study Lab is built around that reality.

  • Bring a PDF, YouTube link, notes or any topic.
  • Generate a focused lesson with the important points clearly separated.
  • Practice questions from the lesson, not random unrelated content.
  • Use mistakes as revision material instead of losing them after the quiz.

Study Lab turns scattered material into notes, quizzes, flashcards, podcasts and mind maps.
